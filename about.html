About


I can tell you the moment that it really started for me. It was nine years ago, when I was a single mom. I remember watching my one year old son sleeping peacefully. I wanted to be a good example for him, provide stability, someone that he can really be proud of. I wanted to do something extraordinary in my life and create a positive impact. This served as the perfect drive and motivation.

Flash forward to the end of 2014 coming into 2015 a lot of changes started to happen. I graduated from the OATC and earned my Web Development Degree. This is where I was able to pin-point my career and find my passion. I was also given recognition and an award from two of my professors for being a successful student. There is a plaque hanging on the Business Technical Wall with my photo and story on it.

Family Life

Still seeing where this road takes me!
